About Us

At Rothbard, we are dedicated to providing secure solutions for holding bitcoin through multisig. As a client, you will be set up to hold your own Bitcoin keys, which means that you have absolute control over your funds. With Rothbard, you can have peace of mind knowing that your funds are safeguarded by our industry standard multisig service without having to worry if we can access your funds.

Our Services

  • MULTISIG WALLET SETUP

    Our team assists in setting up a robust multisig wallet for your bitcoin holdings, ensuring enhanced security and protection against unauthorized access.

  • SECURITY CONSULTATION

    We offer comprehensive security consultation services to help you understand the benefits of multisig and how it can enhance the security of your bitcoin holdings.

  • SIGNING KEY

    Customers may sign up for our Rothbard signature, which they can include in their multisig to prepare for times when they need to fall back on it.
